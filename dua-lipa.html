Dua Lipa's Journey

Dua Lipa's journey to fame is a story of hard work, talent, and persistence. Born in London in 1995 to Albanian parents, Dua was influenced by music from a young age. She started posting song covers on YouTube as a teenager, which helped her gain early attention. At 15, she moved to Kosovo for a short time before returning to London to pursue music seriously.

Dua's Fame Song

"Levitating" is a hit song by Dua Lipa, released in 2020 as part of her album Future Nostalgia. The track blends disco, pop, and funk, creating an upbeat and danceable vibe. Lyrically, the song is about the excitement of new love, using cosmic and space-related imagery to describe a relationship that feels magical and unstoppable.
