Harry Styles's Journey

Harry Styles is a British singer, songwriter, and actor who first gained fame as a member of the global sensation One Direction before launching a successful solo career. His self-titled debut album, released in 2017, introduced a rock and pop-influenced sound with hits like "Sign of the Times." His second album, Fine Line (2019), solidified his status as a major solo artist with tracks like "Watermelon Sugar" and "Adore You." In 2022, his third album, Harry’s House, featured the record-breaking hit "As It Was," which topped charts worldwide. Known for his charismatic stage presence, unique fashion style, and genre-blending music, Harry Styles has become one of the most influential and celebrated artists of his generation.

Harry's Fame Song

"As It Was" is a song by Harry Styles, released on April 1, 2022, as the lead single from his third studio album, Harry’s House. The song features an upbeat yet melancholic sound, blending synth-pop and ’80s-inspired production with introspective lyrics about change, nostalgia, and personal struggles.
