Ed Sheeran's journey to fame is a story of passion, perseverance, and raw talent. Born in Halifax, England, in 1991, he started playing guitar and writing songs at a young age. In his teens, he moved to London to pursue music, performing at small gigs and self-releasing EPs. His breakthrough came in 2010 when he uploaded his music online, catching the attention of Jamie Foxx and Elton John
